    Central University of Kerala BA Admission 2021

    Here we are providing the details of full-time three years, Bachelor of Arts course at UG level offered by the college. The minimum eligibility for admission in BA is a pass in 10+2 with at least 50% marks in aggregate. The selection of the candidates is based on merit in the qualifying examination and score in CU CET.

    Name of Course Bachelor of Arts
    Duration 3 Years
    Seat Intake 50
    Specialization International Relations
    Mode of Application Online
    Minimum Eligibility Candidates must have Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) qualified from a recognized board of education with Mathematics subject

    Eligibility Criteria

    • Candidates must have at least 50% marks or equivalent in Plus Two from a recognized Board (studied in 10+2 system).Up to 5% relaxation in marks for SC/ST candidates.
    • The candidates should not have completed 19 years of age.

    Selection Process

    • In order to get admission, eligible candidates need to sit for a National level Entrance exam known as Central University Common Entrance Test (CU CET-2020) organized and conducted by the ten Central Universities together.
    • Based on the rank obtained and college selected, seat allotment will be done
    • Selected candidates will be informed through a call.
    • All the selected candidates need to report at the college for further admission process along with valid documents.

    Eligibility Criteria

    • Master’s Degree in the concerned /allied/relevant subject with a minimum of 55% marks, or with a CGPA of 6.0 in the 10 point scale in the qualifying examination, from a University recognized by the University Grants Commission.
    • Up to 5% relaxation in the minimum requirement of marks is granted to SC/ST candidates.

    Selection Process

    • Candidates have to apply and appear for the CU CET Exam 2021.
    • Candidates who have qualified UGC/ National/NET/ shall be exempted from entrance examination of P.hD and shall be eligible directly for appearing in Personal Interview.
    • The selection of candidates will be made on merit prepared from marks obtained in the entrance exam, in Personal interview (PI), and in their Post Graduation examination.
    • Selected candidates will have to present in Group Discussion and Personal Interview.
    • Selected candidates can take admission on the required date.
    • For admission to Ph.D. Program, the candidates working in CUK Research Projects and those holding Financial Assistance from Govt. of India/Rajiv Gandhi National Fellowship (RGNF)/Maulana Azad National Fellowship (MANF), etc. are required to pass in the written entrance test as well as Interview
    • The candidates having any financial assistance such as RGNF, MANF, etc., will be given priority over those without any financial assistance, subject to qualifying entrance test.

    How to Apply for Central University of Kerala Admissions 2021?

    • Candidates need to apply online only. For online application, candidates need to visit the homepage of CUCET
    • On successful online application submission with online/debit card/credit card successful payment, an automated e-mail and SMS will be sent to the mail id & mobile number you have given in the application form.
    • For online application and payment made through “Challan”, candidates may have to wait for 7 days and if they do not receive it within 7 days, they may send an e-mail to CUCET e-mail address queries@cucet.co.in.
    • Take a printout for the same as future reference.

    Note: Priority in P.hD seats will be given to candidates with JRF/NET. They have to apply for CUCET 2018 but candidates with JRF are exempted from appearing for CUCET 2018 in CU Kerala.

    FAQs

    Ques. On what criteria college provides scholarships?

    Ans. College basically provides scholarships to the meritorious students. This is done in order to create an environment of healthy competition among the students. College also offers some fee concession to the students from economically weaker backgrounds.

    Ques. Can the course fee be paid in installments per year?

    Ans. The University has provided an option to pay the fee for every year during its beginning as per college fee structure.

    Ques. Does the college provide Hostel facilities to their students?

    Ans. Yes, Hostel facilities have been provided for boys and girls. It provides students with delicious food and provides options too.

    Ques. What will be asked in CUCET Entrance exams for LLM?

    Ans. Common entrance conducted for students interested in law will have questions on general English, general knowledge and constitution and jurisdiction among other law-related subjects at UG level.

    Ques. How much is the application fee for the CUCET application?

    Ans. The CUCET application fee is as follows:

    1. General/ OBC category: INR 800
    2. SC/ ST category: INR 350
    3. PwD category: No charges

    Ques. Is there any seat reservation policy at CUK?

    Ans. Yes, seats are available for reserved categories for all the courses, 15% of seats are reserved for SC, 7.5% for ST, 27% for non-creamy layer OBC candidates.

    Ques. What are the basic criteria to be eligible for the MPH program at CUK?

    Ans. The candidates with a minimum aggregate of 50 percent in graduation (any discipline) are eligible to apply for the MPH program at CUK.

    Ques. How are admissions done at CUK?

    Ans. CUK admissions for both UG and PG programmes are based on scores in the Central Universities Common Entrance Test (CUCET). And admission to the Ph.D. program is based on a valid score in UGC NET followed by an Interview round conducted by the University.

    Ques. How will I know that my application has reached the admission section?

    Ans. It will be done through the Online Application, on the successful online application submission with fee payment through resources like Net-banking/Debit Card /Credit Card/UPI, an automated email and SMS will be sent to the e-mail ID & mobile number the candidate had given in the application form.

    Ques. What are the eligibility criteria to apply for the BA programme?

    Ans. The candidates must have passed Class 12 or equivalent in relevant discipline with a minimum aggregate of 50 percent to apply for the BA program at CUK.

    Ques. Are scholarships offered to students at CUK?

    Ans. Yes, the students are offered the scholarships, which are:

    • Postgraduate Indira Gandhi Scholarship for single girl child pursuing a PG level program offered by the University. A scholarship of INR 3,100 per month is given to such students.
    • The Government of Kerala Scholarship to SC/ ST students for overseas studies.

    Ques. Are vehicles allowed inside the campus after the admission?

    Ans. The use of vehicles or parking of vehicles is prohibited inside the campus except for physically challenged students, who can use their carrier wheelchair with the permission of the registrar. 

    Students are encouraged to use cycles or solar bikes. If not, they may park their vehicles near the entrance of the campus. 

    Ques. Are students provided a hostel facility at CUK? If yes, what is the timing of the visiting hours?

    Ans. Yes, the college provides the hostel facility to its students. The visiting hour timing is 5 PM to 7 PM. The hostel gate closes at 8 PM, all the students are instructed to be inside the hostel till 8 PM. 

    Ques. Is hostel money refundable at CUK?

    Ans. No, the hostel money is not refundable, if the money is delayed and not paid on time, then a fine of INR 50 is to be paid on a daily basis. ‘However, an amount of INR 2000 as caution money will be given back to the students at the time of leaving the hostel which was taken at the starting of the session.
